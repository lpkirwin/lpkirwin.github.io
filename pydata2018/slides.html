Small data problems, not
big data problems

  • Smaller numbers of larger clients
  • Extreme class imbalances and skewed distributions
  • Claims inflation and slow development

Choosing your targets

  • Accuracy is not the same thing as profitability
  • Key is to find informative factors that are not currently properly priced
  • You'll probably want to use custom loss functions
Model MSE MAE
Naive (sample mean) 0.0738 0.1942

Factor 2 (fleet related) 0.0716 0.1812
Factor 3 (past performance) 0.0829 0.1723
Loss ratio = Claims (£) / Premiums (£)

The importance of storytelling

  • Business doesn't care about your AUC
  • In the face of uncertainty, you need a clear narrative about why and how your model works
  • ELI5 / LIME / Shaply packages are great for this, but low-tech solutions can work as well
